# Writing course.json

Every course begins with a single file: `course.json`. This file sits at the root of your course folder and acts as the entry point for Course Imports. When the app opens a course, `course.json` is the first file it reads. It tells the app what the course is called, who wrote it, and — critically — which topics to load and in what order.

Think of `course.json` as the cover page and table of contents rolled into one. Without it, the app has no way to know what your folder contains.
